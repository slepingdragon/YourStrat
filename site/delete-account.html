Delete your account and data

Applies to the YourStrat app · Last updated: May 26, 2026

You can delete your YourStrat account and all of its data at any time. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Delete from inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open YourStrat and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and data are removed immediately and you are signed out.

Can't access the app?

Email baniabradyy@gmail.com from the email address on your account and ask us to delete it. We verify the request and complete deletion within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes everything tied to it:

  • Your account and email/login credential
  • Your profile and health & fitness details (units, weight, height, age, sex, activity, goals, calorie/macro targets)
  • Your nutrition logs — meals, food items, calories and macros, and any meal photos
  • Your workout logs — routines, exercises, sessions, sets/reps/weights, and effort ratings
  • Operational records such as daily scan counts and trial state

What is kept, and for how long

We keep no personal data after your account is deleted. Meal photos are sent to Google's Gemini API only at the moment of a scan to estimate nutrition; they are not stored on our servers. Any residual copies in routine, encrypted backups are overwritten within 30 days. We retain no analytics or advertising data — the app uses none.
